Who Are We?

Waterfall Chartered Accountants Chambers represents South Africa’s most innovative approach to professional services: independent CA(SA) firms united under shared governance standards without sacrificing ownership or identity.

Our member firms combine collective resources and national reach with the agility and accountability that only independent practices can deliver. Every engagement meets consistent governance, quality, and transformation standards backed by specialist expertise across audit, advisory, forensics, ICT audit, actuarial, and fintech.

Why Join Waterfall Chambers

Become part of South Africa’s premier federated chambers of CA(SA) firms, where independence meets collective scale. As a member firm, you retain full ownership while gaining access to national reach, shared governance standards, and transformation leadership credentials that strengthen competitiveness in both public and private sector markets.

Our Services

External & Internal Audit

Independent IRBA-compliant external and internal audits ensuring transparency, governance maturity, and audit readiness support for public and private sector entities.

Tax & Accounting Support

Tax consulting, regulatory compliance, accounting support, and audit readiness tailored to South Africa’s evolving business environment.

Corporate Finance & Transactions

Transaction advisory (buy-side / sell-side), capital raising, debt & equity advisory, valuations, and project finance solutions that enable growth and investment readiness.

Management Consulting
