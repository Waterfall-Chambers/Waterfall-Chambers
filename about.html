Who Are We?

Waterfall Chartered Accountants Chambers represents South Africa’s most innovative approach to professional services: independent CA(SA) firms united under shared governance standards without sacrificing ownership or identity.

Our member firms combine collective resources and national reach with the agility and accountability that only independent practices can deliver. Every engagement meets consistent governance, quality, and transformation standards backed by specialist expertise across audit, advisory, forensics, ICT audit, actuarial, and fintech.

The Chambers Advantage
  • ✔ Independent Expertise: firms retain full ownership and autonomy
  • ✔ Collective Credibility: governance aligned with King IV & IRBA AQI standards
  • ✔ National Capability: provincial coverage through a federated model
  • ✔ Quality Assurance:standardized excellence without corporate overhead

Our Journey

2022

Waterfall Chartered Accountants Chambers was founded by a team of CA(SA) professionals, engineers, and strategic leaders with a mission to create South Africa's premier professional chambers model. From the outset, our vision was to unite independent firms under shared governance standards while preserving autonomy and ownership.

2023

Expanded our membership network across multiple provinces, establishing standardized governance, quality, and compliance frameworks aligned with King IV principles and IRBA AQI standards. Early transformation initiatives, including diversity targets and B-BBEE alignment, were introduced.

2024

Secured our first corporate clients and launched our flagship platform to provide actionable insights for independent professionals and member firms. The platform enabled national reach, while reinforcing chambers-based governance and professional specialization across audit, advisory, and forensic services.

2025

Solidified transformation leadership with a Level 1 B-BBEE collective and measurable diversity achievements. Expanded specialized services in fintech, actuarial, ICT audit, and regulatory compliance, while emphasizing the Chambers advantage: independent expertise with collective scale and consistent quality standards.

2026 & Beyond

Continue scaling nationally through the federated chambers model, enhancing member firm capabilities, fostering authentic transformation partnerships, and delivering future-focused, professional services while maintaining the independence and identity of each member firm.
